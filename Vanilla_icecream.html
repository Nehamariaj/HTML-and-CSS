Homemade Vanilla Icecream

Homemade vanilla ice cream is a simple treat made with cream, milk, sugar, and vanilla. It's easy to customize and tastes better than store-bought.

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Whole milk
  • Cane sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Chill a large bowl and beaters in the freezer for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In the chilled bowl, whip the heavy cream with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.
  3. Gently fold the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract into the whipped cream until just combined.
  4. Pour the mixture into an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours, or until firm.
  5. Scoop and enjoy with your favorite toppings!
