MY 3 FAVOURITE SONGS

Hello people!!In this web page I am listing out my 3 most loved songs of all time...I can't stop hearing them at all..I hope you all guys like them :)

Munbe Vaa

"Munbe Vaa" is a beautiful and soulful Tamil song from the 2006 film "Sillunu Oru Kaadhal." It's a duet sung by Naresh Iyer and Shreya Ghoshal, composed by A.R. Rahman, and written by Vairamuthu. The song expresses the longing and love between two individuals, with lyrics that evoke deep emotions and imagery. It's considered one of Rahman's most popular and enduring compositions.

Nanare

"Nanare" is a popular Hindi song from the 2007 Bollywood film "Guru." Composed by A.R. Rahman with lyrics by Gulzar, it's sung by Shreya Ghoshal. The song is known for its beautiful melody and Shreya Ghoshal's soulful vocals. It's picturized on Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and depicts a woman's journey of self-discovery and empowerment. "Nanare" has become a fan favorite and is often praised for its lyrical depth and emotional resonance.

Ethrayo Janmamayi

"Ethrayo Janmamayi" is a melodious Malayalam song from the 1998 film "Summer in Bethlehem." Composed by Vidyasagar and sung by Sujatha Mohan, the song is a beautiful expression of love and longing. The lyrics, written by Gireesh Puthenjeri, speak of a deep connection that transcends time and space.
